Violin

I started playing the violin in fourth grade and used this instrument to express my feelings through the power of music. My nine-years experience in orchestra, which includes four years with the esteemed Orchestra of Arcadia in California and my position as concertmaster in ninth grade, has not only improved my musical ability but has also fostered my aptitude for careful listening and cooperative working as being part of it.

Repertoire Selection: Concerto No. 1 in E Major, op. 8, RV 269 "La Primavera" (Spring), allegro by A. Vivaldi

Music selection: Baroque

Repertoire Selection: Concerto No. 2 in G Major, allegro by F. J. Haydn

Music selection: Classical
