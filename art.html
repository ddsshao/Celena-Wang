ART

Welcome to my love for art.



From the age of three, art became my devoted friend and an observer throughout my life. Art allowed me to capture and perpetually preserve the essence of landscapes and souls in many ways.





Traditional Chinese Painting国画

My passion for the world of art was kindled through Traditional Chinese Painting (国画 “Guo Hua”) when I attended an international elementary school in Shanghai, China​​​​​​​. I started learning this art when I had just flown from Los Angeles to Shanghai, knowing very little about my own culture. Traditional Chinese Painting ushered me into the realm of my Chinese heritage and sparked a strong fascination of love ever since then where I later became my granduncle’s art assistant, as he was a Traditional Chinese Painter, further deepening my connection to this captivating art form.
